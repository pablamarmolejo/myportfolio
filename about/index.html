¡Hola!

Hello, my name is Pabla Marmolejo. I am a UX/UI designer based in Barcelona. I am currently studying for a Master’s Degree in Digital Experience Design at BAU.

My skills range from UI design, UX research to frontend (HTML and CSS), and UX writing. I like to think of myself as a jack of all trades: Besides UX/UI design, I’ve also studied translation and languages (Spanish, English, and French).

I like working with companies that bring innovation to the picture, and that are not afraid to challenge themselves. I am a creative and self-determined person, but I am first and foremost a dreamer.
